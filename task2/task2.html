RATAN TATA

Ratan Tata's Life in brief :--

  • 28 December 1937

    • Ratan Tata was born in Bombay, now Mumbai, during the British Raj, on 28 December 1937.[6] He is the son of Naval Tata, who was born in Surat and later adopted into the Tata family, and Sooni Tata, the niece of Tata group founder Jamsetji Tata.

  • Ratan tata's Education

    • His early education was completed at Campion School, for further studies he went to the USA, where he did his BS in Cornell University Architecture, after which he joined Harvard Business School where he earned a degree in Advanced Management.

  • Ratan Tata's Career

    • Tata worked briefly at Jones & Emmons in Los Angeles, California before returning to India. His intention was to settle in California, America, but when his grandmother's health deteriorated in India, he had to come to India and after coming to India, he worked with IBM. But his uncle JRD Tata did not like that the son of his house should work in another company.

  • Ratan Tata's Assets

    • Ratan Tata's assets are 1 billion i.e. 7740 thousand crores in Indian Rupees. The biggest thing is that Ratan Tata gives a large amount of money in the form of donations to various types of social organizations. So that the people of the lower level present in the society can get proper development. Apart from this, many types of charitable trusts are run by the Tata company. When the Corona epidemic came to India, a donation of ₹ 500 crores was made by the Tata Group to the Prime Minister's PM Cares Fund.
